Welcome to the general page

Superheroes are fictional characters typically depicted as possessing extraordinary abilities or superpowers, and they often use these powers to protect society from various threats, such as supervillains, criminals, and natural disasters. Superheroes are a popular and enduring genre in comic books, movies, television shows, and other forms of media. Superheroes have been a part of popular culture for decades, and they continue to capture the imagination of audiences worldwide with their exciting adventures, compelling stories, and enduring themes of heroism, courage, and sacrifice.
